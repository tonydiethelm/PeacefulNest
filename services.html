As a postpartum doula I can...

  • provide non-medical, physical, emotional, educational and family support after the birth of your baby
  • assist you with self-care recovery measures
  • provide information and education about baby care
  • assist you in caring for and feeding your new baby
  • offer basic lactation support
  • assist your family with adjusting to new roles
  • pick up items from the pharmacy or store
  • offer household support such as preparing occasional light meals, sweeping, grocery shopping, laundry, tidying and dish-washing so you can nap, shower, or attend to other self-care tasks
  • give foot rubs :)
  • prepare a hot pack or draw a bath
